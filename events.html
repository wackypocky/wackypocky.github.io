17 nov 2018 -- Alfred Lerner Hall, Columbia University

Collaboration with Columbia Classical Performers

Musicians from PCMS and from Columbia’s own chamber music organization will collaborate on a concert event in NYC, deepening ties between the two universities and opening up chances for mutually enriching chamber music experiences.

30 nov 2018 -- Visual & Performing Arts, Trenton Central High School

Tigers-in-Trenton

PCMS hosts the first ever "Tigers-in-Trenton" concert, bringing a diverse array of student music groups from Princeton to Trenton to perform for and speak with members of the Trenton Youth Orchestra.

28 mar 2019 -- Princeton University Art Museum

Gainsborough’s England

In collaboration with the Princeton University Art Museum’s spring exhibit on the English artist Thomas Gainsborough (1746-1788), PCMS will present a program of English music from Gainsborough’s time to the 19th Century for an exciting interdisciplinary concert event.
