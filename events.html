9 nov 2019 -- Chancellor Green Rotunda, Princeton University

Verklärte Nacht

In our fourth collaboration with the German Department, PCMS will explore the intensely emotional world of spiritual metamorphosis inside Arnold Schönberg’s Verklärte Nacht, featuring a reading of Richard Dehmel’s eponymous poem that provided the inspiration for this transformative string sextet.

21 nov 2019 -- Princeton University Art Museum

Being Sound: Music, Madness, and Medicine

In collaboration with the Princeton University Art Museum’s exhibition, Worlds of Care: Visualizing Illness and Healing, PCMS will journey into the multifaceted intersection of music and medicine. The program, which spans six centuries, ranges from a musical parody of a surgery to works that have been integrated into clinical music therapy.
